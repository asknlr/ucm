Welcome to the website of UCM Global Limited. At UCM Global Limited, we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe and secure online experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

Information we collect
  1. Personal Information: When you use our website, you may be asked to provide personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and other relevant details necessary for us to provide you with the requested services.
  2. Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the time and date of your visit.
  3. Cookies: We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand how you interact with our website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  1. To provide you with the services you request, such as visa assistance and job placement.
  2. To respond to your inquiries and requests.
  3. To improve our website's functionality and user experience.
  4. To send you promotional materials, updates, and newsletters if you have opted in to receive them.
  5. To analyze and improve our marketing and advertising strategies.
Data Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers or partners who assist us in providing our services. These third parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. Please check this page periodically for updates.
