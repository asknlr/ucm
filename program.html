  • WORK AND TRAVEL

    Unlock the world with our dynamic Work & Travel programs! Dive into the heart of a new culture while offsetting your travel costs â€“ working abroad is the key! Our programs offer a unique blend of earning opportunities, daily life experiences, and cultural exploration. Immerse yourself in local culture, make lifelong friends, and enhance your language skills while gaining valuable work experience. We've got you covered with job placement support for a worry-free adventure.

    Program Highlights:
    Embark on the Summer Work and Travel program, an exchange visitor initiative to the United States, igniting cultural exchange and understanding. Created under the Mutual Education and Cultural Exchange Act of 1961, this program facilitates the interchange of knowledge and skills in education, arts, sciences, and business management.

    For Nigerian Students:
    This program provides an exciting chance for Nigerian students to engage in educational and cultural experiences in the United States. Under the Work and Travel category, participants can contribute their skills and experiences upon their return.

    To participate in this program, the applicant must be:

    1. Be between the ages of 18 and 28.
    2. Be a full time university student with at least one completed semester of postsecondary study.
    3. Demonstrate an intermediate level of English proficiency.
    4. Provide two references showcasing maturity and the ability to maximize program benefits.
    5. Submit medical and police reports, agreeing total background check.
    6. Present a copy of the "personal details" page of their international passport.
    7. Provide a valid email address for ongoing communication.
    8. Attend all required one on one interviews.
    9. Submit a one page essay on how an international program would benefit them upon returning to their home country.
    10. Supply a school letter on official letterhead confirming current full time student status.
    11. Provide a parental consent letter verified by UCM global.


    If you meet these criteria, take the first step toward your global adventure! Apply on our site to embark on a transformative journey of work, travel, and cultural discovery.
